Research Focus

Adrenergic Receptors

Exploring the role of alpha-1A adrenergic receptors in regulating cardiac metabolism and protecting against cardiometabolic disease.

Cardiotoxicity

Investigating cardiovascular complications of targeted cancer therapies, particularly kinase inhibitors, to improve patient outcomes.

Nuclear Receptors

Understanding the adaptive functions of ROR-alpha in maintaining cardiac mitochondrial function and mitigating heart failure progression.

About the Lab

The Jensen Lab at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill investigates the molecular and pathophysiological mechanisms underlying heart failure. Our translational research approach bridges fundamental biology with clinical application, driven by active clinical practice in advanced heart failure cardiology.
