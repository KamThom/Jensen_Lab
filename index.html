About the Lab

The Jensen Lab investigates the molecular and pathophysiological mechanisms of heart failure using both in vitro and in vivo models. Our work is informed by Dr. Brian Jensen’s clinical practice as an advanced heart failure cardiologist, ensuring a translational approach that bridges basic science and patient care.

Our focus areas include mitochondrial function, adrenergic signaling, cardio-oncology, and the role of nuclear receptors in cardiac physiology. The lab collaborates with faculty across UNC’s Schools of Medicine, Pharmacy, Public Health, and Engineering.
